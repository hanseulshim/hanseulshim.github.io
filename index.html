Hi, I'm Hanseul Shim

I am a self taught full-stack web developer using the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.JS, React.JS, Node.JS) as well as HTML5, CSS, and Bootstrap. I am comfortable with both working on the front end and back end and prefer working with both ends in developing applications.

I graduated from the University of Texas at Austin in 2014 with a degree in Electrical Engineering. Feel free to check out my projects below or contact me for any opportunites.
